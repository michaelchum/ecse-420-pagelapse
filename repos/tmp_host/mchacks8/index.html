FAQ

Judging Criteria

These are the criteria that the judges will be using to rate your hacks at the end. Use these to help develop a sense of what you want to be developing.

Design/Polish

How well designed is the hack? Is the user experience good? How buggy does it appear? For this one it's really the details that matter. Make sure that you've thought through the kinks even if you can't solve all of them.

Technical Impressiveness

How difficult of a problem does the hack address? How much engineering work went into it? Did you shoot for the moon? Did you end up in the stars?

Practicality

The most fun part of hacking! This category really boils down to would you use this again in a week? It doesn't need to be a business but is it actually helpful for anything?
